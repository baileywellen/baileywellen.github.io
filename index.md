# Data for more equitable health care.

<p class="lede">I am a data scientist studying addiction health services and public policy, with a focus on treatment access, implementation barriers, and responsible use of health data.</p>

I earned undergraduate degrees in Computer Science and Data Science from [Carthage College](https://www.carthage.edu/) and am now pursuing a Ph.D. in Computational and Data Sciences at [Washington University in St. Louis](https://wustl.edu/). My work uses electronic health records and administrative data to identify disparities in substance use disorder treatment access. I care deeply about algorithmic fairness, implementation feasibility, and making research relevant to people with lived experience.
