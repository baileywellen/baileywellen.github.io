# Teaching

<p class="section-intro">Supporting thoughtful, responsible practice in data science.</p>

I enjoy helping students connect technical skills with the human questions that make those skills meaningful. My teaching interests include data science, statistical thinking, algorithmic fairness, and the responsible use of data in health-care settings.

I aim to create learning experiences that invite students to question assumptions, recognize the limitations of data, and build tools with care for the people affected by them.
